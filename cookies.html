Cookies Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function correctly and provide a better user experience by remembering your preferences.

2. HOW WE USE COOKIES

Since kennloner.com is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages, we do not directly set proprietary tracking cookies. However, technical cookies may be used by the hosting provider for security and system administration.

3. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

Our website features links to music streaming services and marketing tools. When you click on a link to Spotify, Apple Music, HearNow, or Show.co, these platforms may set their own cookies to track conversions or provide analytics. We recommend reviewing their respective cookie policies for more details.

4. MANAGING COOKIES

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your ability to interact with some of the external links on our site.

5. ANALYTICS

We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors interact with our content. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the Kenn Loner digital experience.

6. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page.
